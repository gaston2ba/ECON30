# Paris arrondissement HDI methodology

UN HDR 2022: geometric mean of life expectancy, education, and income indices.
PPP conversion: EUR / 0.738 = 2017 intl $ PPP.
Life expectancy: ORS-IDF / Institut Paris Région 2021 anchors; others income-interpolated.
Education: MYS/EYS proxies from Filosofi income rank (diploma tables not in pipeline).
