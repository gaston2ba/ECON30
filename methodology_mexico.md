# Mexico City HDI methodology

Source: PNUD México *Informe sobre Desarrollo Humano Municipal* (municipal IDH series).
Raw transcription: `data/mexico/raw/hdi_mexico_pnud.csv`.
Values used: **2020** municipal HDI for the 16 CDMX alcaldías.
Reference: https://hdr.undp.org/data-center/specific-country-data and PNUD Mexico municipal reports.
Sub-indices (life, education, income) are not published per alcaldía in this extract; only composite HDI.
